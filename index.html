Hosthelper

This app helps you monitor processes on your pc and on other computers on your local network

You can create remotes, view processes on them simultaneously and also create your own buttons that do specific commands

The intent is for this application to function as a convenient GUI to get an overview of your various devices and processes without having to ump between terminals and windows and look up your various SSH login information
